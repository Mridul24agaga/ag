Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28th July 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Morley Marketing Consultants Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected in compliance with the Data Protection Act 2018 and UK GDPR.

We are the data controller responsible for your personal data.

What Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Identity Data: name, company name (if applicable)
  • Contact Data: email address, phone number
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, time zone settings, and other technology on the devices you use to access this website
  • Usage Data: information about how you use our website
  • Marketing and Communication Data: preferences in receiving marketing from us

How We Collect Your Data

We collect your data through:

  • Direct interactions: You may provide personal information by filling out forms or corresponding with us via email.
  • Automated technologies: We may use cookies, server logs, and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and manage our services
  • Respond to enquiries or support requests
  • Send important notices or service updates
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

We will only use your personal data where we are legally permitted to do so. Common lawful bases include consent, performance of a contract, legal obligation, and legitimate interest.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted service providers (e.g., website hosting, email platforms) who help us operate our business and only under data processing agreements.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring your information is secure. We have put in place suitable technical and organisational measures to prevent unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including any legal, accounting, or reporting obligations.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (if processing was based on consent)

To exercise your rights, email us at team@morleyconsultants.com.

Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies. For detailed information, refer to our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites, plug-ins, or applications. We are not responsible for their privacy statements or data handling practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
